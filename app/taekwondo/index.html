Tae Kwon Do

TaeKwonDo is the most popular martial art practiced in the world today. It teaches a variety of direct and controlled hand techniques to complement efficient and powerful kicks. USTA classes in TaeKwonDo are taught to students of all levels. And students may begin instruction as early as 5 years old.﻿

About USTA Martial Arts

USTA Martial Arts for Men, Women, and Children.

Our Program for Children is More Than Just Kicks and Punches.

Our USTA program includes group, private, and semi-private lessons in Taekwondo for all ages,form young children to adult. Through USTA, students learn martial arts techniques and self-defense skills while developing self-confidence and discipline.﻿

It is a comprehensive program to help promote your child’s mental and physical development, enhancing :

Discipline
Respect
Self-Confidence
Balance
Coordination
Strength
